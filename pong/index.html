    How to play

    Enter in your name and when the other player is ready click on the screen to play, first to three wins!


    Controls

    To control the paddle simple move the mouse within the gamescreen aka the balck box.


    By clicking on the chat box at the bottom of the screen after starting the game you will be able to chat and play at the same time by pressing enter after writing a message.
